Day Camp 2014

Day Camp will be hosted at Camp Kiesel on August 7 for all Cub Scouts and Webelos.

The cost of camp is $28 and will include a pack t-shirt to wear to camp. We'll meet at the chapel at 6:30 and leave by 6:45 AM on the 7th. We should be back around 3PM.

  • Cubs must bring their own sack lunch
  • Re-fillable water bottle
  • Sunscreen and bug repellant
  • Hiking boots are recommended
  • Weather appropriate clothing
Some spending money is encouraged for use at the trading post, although not required.
